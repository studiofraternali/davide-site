Biography

Independent italian author, born in Rimini. He lives in Montefiore Conca, in Emilia-Romagna. In the 90s he attended the School of Art in Riccione, later graduating from the University of Urbino with a degree in communication and advertising. In 2015 he attended Silvano Agosti's cinema course, in Rome.
